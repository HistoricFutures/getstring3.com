How does String3 work?

String3 asks preprogrammed questions (StringQuestions) about product provenance and routes them to the organisations in your supply chain.

Question Progress Report

Your question progress report gives you detailed information about the supply chain for every product that you ask about. All you need to do is send your question and it builds itself.

For each key production stage, valuable information about the suppliers involved is displayed, including how many different suppliers were involved, where they are located and how good they are at responding to questions.

  • Each question is sent about a specific instance of supply so you can track changes to your supply chains accurately over time

  • Suppliers you don’t have a direct relationship with are anonymised but have a unique reference number so you can keep track of their appearance in your supply chain

  • You can see what part each supplier played in the manufacture of your product, or if they haven’t responded to your enquiries

  • This shows where this specific instance of supply of your product was mined

Researched Process Maps

The experts behind String3 research the materials and processes behind the products you sell. This means we’ve done a lot of the hard work for you, defining the main stages involved in transforming raw materials into finished products. Your StringQuestions will flow easily throughout the supply chain using one of these Process Maps.

We update our Process Maps all the time. If you don’t see the material you need, simply email us and we’ll work with you to add it.

The Question Builder

Getting a question out of the door with String3 couldn’t be easier. Using our ready to use question templates, you simply add the details of the specific instance of supply of your product, details of your supplier and an email is generated automatically.

On our enterprise membership plan you can even connect String3 to your existing systems using our secure API. Our systems consultants can help you set that up.

How it works for suppliers

String3 works by asking a series of simple questions to your suppliers. All they have to do is answer whether they made or sourced each material in the process and pass it on to their suppliers, until you reach the organisation that can reliably answer the question.

They can answer with confidence because their information is never shared with you but always kept confidential and anonymised.
