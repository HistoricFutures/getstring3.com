Pricing

You can ask up to 12 questions a year about your supply chains for free. If you need more, you can upgrade once you're using String3. We never charge suppliers to respond to your questions.

Register for free

Standard

£720 per year (Excl. VAT)

36 questions per year

If you are worried about specific issues such as modern slavery or deforestation and need to start identifying which of your products are putting your brand at the most risk, our Standard membership is designed for you. Ask 36 questions to get more detailed reports on question progress and supplier status.

Premium

£5400 per year (Excl. VAT)

360 questions per year

If your organisation needs to manage risks within a broader product portfolio, String3 Premium membership provides a higher volume of questions per year (360), advanced reporting and voice and video support to help you get all the information you need.
