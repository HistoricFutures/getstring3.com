Brands and retailers are faced with a proliferation of risks related to their global supply chains. Modern Slavery, deforestation, chemical usage, animal welfare - to name but a few. Managing these risks and carrying out due diligence without knowledge about your supply chains is a huge challenge.

With better information about where your supply chain operates, you know what risks might be present in your supply chain.

String3 is a new, online, real time supply chain mapping tool designed for companies to ask questions about where and how their products are made, supporting both reputational and regulatory risk management activities.

Join our webinar to see a live demo of String3 to see how it anonymously connects the nodes of a supply chain together allowing suppliers to respond to questions knowing their confidential customer and supplier data is never shared.
